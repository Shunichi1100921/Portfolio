Research 2024 - Present

LC/MS Data Analysis via Mixture-of-Mixtures Model Leveraging Sample Hierarchy

Developing an analysis tool that models complex LC/MS signal data as mixture distributions to identify and quantify proteins and peptides. Proposed a Mixture-of-Mixtures model that exploits sample hierarchy, and implemented an optimization algorithm using optimal transport as the objective function.

Python Statistical modeling Proteomics EM algorithm

Gene Network Extraction with LLMs

Targeted 19 genes related to breast cancer KEGG Pathway. Performed pairwise edge estimation with an LLM and integrated results into a DAG. Improved performance to F1=0.780 through iterative prompt design, outperforming statistical methods.
